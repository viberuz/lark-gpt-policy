Privacy Policy for LARK GPT

LARK GPT, an AI-powered financial advisor based on ARK-style research methodologies, does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information.

API data (such as stock prices and news) is fetched in real-time from third-party providers like Alpha Vantage, Polygon.io, and Indian Stock APIs solely to enhance your experience. We do not store or resell this data.

For questions, please contact the maintainer at contact@larkgpt.ai.
